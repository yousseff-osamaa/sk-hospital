About Souad Kafafi University Hospital

At MUST, we recognise the significance of medicine and wellness, which is why our university hospital was established with rigorous standards to cater to the needs of patients.

The Souad Kafafi University Hospital (SKUH) offers a comprehensive range of hospital and wellness services, combining convenience with award-winning clinical excellence. With over 300 affiliated doctors specializing in more than 40 clinical fields, the hospital ensures top-notch medical care.

Moreover, the hospital creates an ideal learning environment for both junior and senior medical students, allowing them to practice medicine most effectively. Patients and their families receive exceptional medical and hospitality services, ensuring their comfort and well-being.

Additionally, the hospital features Centres of Excellence along with advanced facilities, including outpatient clinics, emergency department, intensive care unit, operating rooms, laboratory, blood bank, inpatient clinics, post-trauma care unit, stroke care unit, isolation unit, ESWL lithotripsy unit, dermatology and laser unit, and many more.

Our Mission

To deliver patient-centered healthcare of the highest standard — combining advanced medical expertise, modern technology, and compassionate care to ensure the well-being of every patient.
